# Canonical Intake and Personalization Layer

## Purpose

The intake side of the project is supposed to do four jobs:
1. map symptom burden
2. map functional damage
3. identify maintaining factors and real-world constraints
4. decide the smallest useful starting system

## Short intake

The short questionnaire asks about:
- what depression feels like on an average day
- what part of life is taking the biggest hit
- what symptom causes the most damage
- what makes recovery harder in real life
- what others misunderstand
- what has helped even a little
- what tone the person can still hear
- what the intro needs to do first
- how much time and energy is actually available
- one sentence they need to hear so they do not tune out

This is not diagnostic. It is a personalization intake.

## Intake interpretation logic

The guide converts answer patterns into response strategy:

- **Hopeless / effort feels pointless** -> restore credibility that action can still matter
- **Exhausted / overwhelmed** -> reduce load and explain minimum viable action
- **Shame / self-attack** -> separate state from identity and lower moral pressure
- **Confused / overthinking** -> clarify the model and reduce fog
- **Emotionally flat / disconnected** -> name the syndrome without demanding performance

Tone should be matched deliberately:
- clinical
- practical
- blunt
- emotionally real
- hopeful without fake optimism

Plan size should be matched to actual battery:
- very low -> survival anchors + one visible win
- low to moderate -> core anchors + one practical block
- moderate -> anchors + one task block + one reset + one admin/social step
- strong -> reinforce the base first, do not turn the day into a worthiness test

## Long-form 150-item intake

The reconstructed long-form intake adds 10 sections:

1. present state and functional damage
2. mood, anhedonia, and despair
3. cognition, self-attack, and rumination
4. sleep and circadian disruption
5. energy, body state, and physical drag
6. avoidance, initiation, and routine failure
7. environment, admin, and practical life friction
8. social shrinkage, relationships, and identity
9. treatment fit, medical complexity, and substance factors
10. hope signal, agency, and plan fit

## Working principle

The right use of intake data is not admiration of scores. It is deciding what to tighten first:
- sleep timing
- morning light
- hydration and food
- meds if used
- movement
- environment friction
- written structure
- low-pressure social contact
- treatment fit
- hope / effort reconnection

## Complexity flags already named in the source base

The project itself warns that the following should not be treated as a simple self-management case:
- bipolar or mixed-state history
- psychotic features
- major substance destabilization
- severe self-neglect
- collapse of basic intake
- rapid deterioration

That means the intake layer is also a triage layer, even if the current source base does not yet formalize that process enough.
