## What this document is for

This intake is designed to do four jobs before a plan is personalized: map symptom burden, map functional damage, identify maintaining factors, and identify the smallest useful starting points. The point is not to produce a dramatic life story. The point is to get a usable picture of what is actually breaking, what is feeding it, and what kind of intervention is realistic first.

It is an intake and planning tool, not a stand-alone diagnosis. Depression can overlap with grief, burnout, trauma, substance effects, chronic pain, medical illness, bipolar-spectrum problems, medication side effects, and ordinary defeat after prolonged adversity. Use this to sharpen understanding and planning, not to pretend complexity does not exist.

Answer from the last 2 to 4 weeks unless an item clearly asks about longer patterns. Score from daily reality, not from what sounds dramatic, respectable, or theoretically correct. If several things are true, score the one that is most true most often.

## Response scale

```text
TABLE
0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4
Not at all | Rare / mild | Sometimes / moderate | Often / significant | Almost always / severe
```

## How to use the scores

Higher section totals do not mean you are worse as a person. They mark where the system is most loaded. In this project, high scores should point attention toward the first few levers: sleep or circadian stability, energy and body care, behavior and avoidance, environment friction, social shrinkage, treatment fit, and hope or effort breakdown.

## Boundary line

This form helps tailor a plan. It is not crisis support. If there is immediate danger, inability to care for basic safety, psychotic symptoms, or a history suggesting mania or mixed states, that needs direct clinical evaluation rather than a self-management document alone.

## Source Basis Used for This Rebuild

This preamble preserves the project’s actual framing: depression is broader than sadness; function deserves equal weight with mood; the plan should prioritize real-life anchors and leverage rather than heroic intensity; and the questionnaire is supposed to personalize the message rather than reward polished self-explanation.

The accessible project materials also emphasize that depression can distort perception, timing, effort valuation, self-appraisal, and social interpretation; that avoidance, rumination, shame, environmental friction, and identity fusion can maintain the state; and that repetition matters more than dramatic bursts of motivation.

The broader clinical basis named in the project materials and checked against current official sources includes NICE guideline NG222 on depression in adults, the 2022 VA/DoD Clinical Practice Guideline for Major Depressive Disorder, WHO depression fact sheet material updated in 2025, and NIMH depression educational materials.

## Working principle

The highest-value use of this intake is not to admire scores. It is to decide what to tighten first: sleep timing, morning light, hydration and food, medication consistency if used, movement, environment friction, written structure, low-pressure social contact, treatment fit, or hope and effort reconnection.
