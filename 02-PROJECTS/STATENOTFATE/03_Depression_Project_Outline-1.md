## I. Project thesis

Depression is treated as a systems failure affecting energy, perception, reward prediction, behavior, and function.

Recovery depends on repeated structural corrections rather than inspiration.

## II. Core design principles

Function over mood alone

Startup design over motivation speeches

Repetition over intensity

Anchors over inspiration

State-based execution over one fixed standard
