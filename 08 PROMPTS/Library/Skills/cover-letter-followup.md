# Cover Letter & Followup

## Purpose
Applications without follow-up or tailored cover letters often disappear. This skill produces the minimal high-leverage written artifacts (cover letter, thank-you, check-in message) that keep you visible in the process while respecting variable energy and avoiding perfectionism. It turns volume into pipeline proof.

## When to Use
- Immediately after tailoring a resume for a specific posting (pair with resume-tailoring + apply-today).
- 3-7 days after submitting if no response (low-friction check-in).
- When the job description asks for a cover letter or "why this role" statement.
- For informational outreach or recruiter messages in the PRS or trades space.
- On low-energy days when you can still produce one short, high-quality artifact.

## Core Instructions
1. Use the target job description + your master-bio (PRS cert APS.006470, Eleeo/D2C reliability & de-escalation, Clark And Sons physical/logistics, sober since 2019-11-01 as proof of follow-through).
2. Identify 2-3 specific overlaps (e.g., "helping people in crisis" for PRS roles, "showing up reliably for physical work" for trades).
3. Produce a short, scannable cover letter or message (under 200-250 words for letters; 3-6 sentences for messages).
4. Keep tone professional yet human — direct, no fluff, no "passionate about" clichés unless earned.
5. End with a clear call to action or next step.
6. Provide a "why this is low-friction" note and one tiny proof action (e.g., "saved this draft + updated tracker").

Always separate Ideal (polished, customized letter) from Realistic (good-enough version you can send today).

## Output Format (Bottom Line First)
- **Bottom Line**: Recommendation (send / tweak / skip) + one-sentence rationale.
- **Draft** (ready to copy):
  - Cover letter version (full or key paragraphs).
  - Follow-up / outreach message version.
- **Key Tailoring Points**: Which of your experiences map directly and why they matter for this role.
- **Honesty Check**: Any stretches and how they are framed safely.
- **Next Action & Proof**: Exact step (submit, send, save to tracker) + how to log it as a floor win.

## Context to Inject
- The specific job posting or lead details
- Your current resume bullets or work history highlights
- Master bio constraints and PRS certification
- Current status (runway pressure, energy, recent applications)
- Any previous communication with this employer
