# Deep Research

## Purpose
Turn vague "I should look into this role" into structured, externalized intelligence: what the work actually looks like day-to-day, real pay/hours/barriers in your market, transferable proof from your background (PRS cert, reliability, de-escalation, physical logistics), and concrete next actions. This prevents fantasy or fear-based decisions and generates visible proof that options exist.

## When to Use
- Before committing serious time to a role family (PRS, HVAC, low-voltage, maintenance).
- When a specific company or posting raises questions (what is the culture, turnover, actual training?).
- As preparation for council-strategy on career pivots or big applications.
- When building or updating 5-year vision — ground it in real data.
- After a week of surface-level applications that feel disconnected.

## Core Instructions
1. Clarify the exact research target (role title + location constraints + specific unknowns).
2. Pull from reliable external sources (job boards, company sites, forums like Reddit r/electricians or r/recovery, official cert bodies, local listings) while staying time-boxed.
3. Map your actual assets against the role: PRS training (APS.006470), Eleeo/D2C experience, Clark And Sons physical/logistics work, sobriety timeline as proof of reliability and following procedures.
4. Surface the real constraints and friction (physical demands on low-energy days, shift schedules, pay runway reality, emotional load for PRS, transportation in Cincinnati area).
5. Produce synthesis: Pros/Cons table, 3-5 realistic pathways or companies, key questions to answer in interview or next action, and one visible proof step (e.g., "save 2 leads + one informational message").
6. Explicitly link back to energy scaling and floor protection.

Always separate Ideal Research (deep + multiple sources) from Realistic Research (30-60 min focused session today).

## Output Format (Bottom Line First)
- **Bottom Line**: One sentence on whether this role/family merits deeper pursuit and the #1 insight.
- **Role Reality Snapshot**: Day-in-the-life, hours, pay range (Cincinnati context), training path, emotional/physical demands.
- **Fit Analysis**: Where your background (PRS cert, customer service reliability, physical work) maps strongly vs. gaps/stretches.
- **Options & Pathways**: 3-5 concrete leads, companies, or next certifications with source + why viable now.
- **Risks / Friction**: Honest bottlenecks (energy, money, transport, shame triggers) and how to mitigate.
- **Next Proof Actions**: 1-3 low-friction moves (save postings, draft outreach, update tracker) scaled to energy.
- **Sources Tracked**: Where the data came from for future reference or weekly review.

## Context to Inject
- Current status block + runway pressure
- Master bio + work history + PRS certification details
- Specific role or company name + any known constraints (location, energy, family)
- Recent job_search_tracker entries or prior research notes
- 5-year vision elements if relevant
