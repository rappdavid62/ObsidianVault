## 00_START_HERE.md

# AI Life Coach Friend — Start Here

**What this is:**  
A clean, local Markdown-based operating system for execution. Not therapy. Not cheerleading. A blunt toolkit for job hunting, routine building, money decisions, social translation, project organization, and daily execution. Designed for non-programmers. Upload the whole folder (or key files) into ChatGPT Projects, NotebookLM, Grok, Gemini, or Antigravity and treat it as your persistent brain.

**Read these first (in order):**
1. `01_Master_Instructions.md` — The rules this system runs on.
2. `02_Current_Status_Block.md` — Your live dashboard. Update every morning.
3. `03_User_Profile.md` — Your baseline facts and constraints.

**Daily update ritual (5–10 minutes):**
- Morning: Edit `02_Current_Status_Block.md` with today's real numbers and one non-negotiable.
- Use `12_Templates/morning_brief.md` to generate your plan.
- Evening: Drop 3–5 lines into `11_Trackers/daily_log.md`.
- When stuck on social stuff, paste the situation into `07_Social_Translator.md` format and ask the AI to fill it.
- Job search: Update `11_Trackers/job_search_tracker.md` and `08_Job_Money_Stability.md`.

**What NOT to put here:**
- Long emotional vents or therapy processing (use a separate private journal).
- Anything you wouldn't want synced to Google Drive or visible in plain text.
- Fantasy goals without concrete next actions.
- Passwords, SSNs, or sensitive financial details (keep those in a password manager).

**How to use with AI tools:**
- **ChatGPT / Gemini / Grok:** Upload the entire `AI_Life_Coach_Friend/` folder as a project. Start every prompt with: "You are my AI Life Coach Friend operating from the files in this folder. Bottom line first. Blunt. Realistic. No fluff."
- **NotebookLM:** Upload all .md files. Ask it to synthesize daily plans or job strategies from the current status.
- **Antigravity / custom setups:** Point it at this folder as your single source of truth. The structure is designed to be copied into any context window.

This system reduces cognitive load by forcing clarity on paper (or in text) before you ask an AI for help. Keep it updated and it becomes your external executive function.
