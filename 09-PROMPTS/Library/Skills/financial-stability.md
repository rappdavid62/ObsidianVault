# Financial Stability & Runway

## Purpose
Financial pressure is a major load-bearing system that interacts with energy, job search motivation, and recovery. This skill forces clear visibility into runway (weeks of expenses covered), necessary minimums, and the smallest actions that either extend runway or convert job search activity into income stability — all without shame spirals or denial.

## When to Use
- Weekly or when status block shows runway pressure.
- Before big spending or commitment decisions (use inside council-strategy).
- On low-energy days when "I need to make money moves" but motivation is zero.
- After any income change or unexpected expense.
- As part of systems-audit or weekly-review to keep the money system green.

## Core Instructions
1. Start with real numbers: current cash + reliable income, fixed monthly expenses (rent, utilities, food, transport, meds, minimal), variable costs.
2. Calculate or estimate runway in weeks (be conservative).
3. Identify the non-negotiable floor expenses that must be protected (do not cut these first).
4. Generate realistic next actions scaled to energy: low-friction income moves (applications, side gigs if viable), expense trims that don't damage health/recovery, or status updates to trackers.
5. Link explicitly to job targets (PRS roles, trades) and how they address runway.
6. Define one tiny proof action and how to register it.

Always separate Ideal (full budget overhaul + aggressive applications) from Realistic (protect floor + 1-2 high-leverage money moves today).

## Output Format (Bottom Line First)
- **Bottom Line**: Current runway estimate + the single highest-leverage money action right now.
- **Runway Snapshot**: Weeks covered (conservative), key assumptions.
- **Floor Protection**: Must-keep expenses and why.
- **Realistic Moves** (energy-scaled):
  - Job/career actions that move the needle on income.
  - Minimal expense adjustments (if any).
- **Risks & Buffers**: What could shorten runway fastest and smallest mitigations.
- **Tracking / Proof**: Suggested update to money tracker or status block.

## Context to Inject
- Current status block (especially runway weeks and one non-negotiable)
- Recent income/expense data or job_search_tracker
- Master bio constraints (location, transportation, recovery needs)
- Target roles and their realistic timelines/pay
- Any active systems-audit or weekly-review output
