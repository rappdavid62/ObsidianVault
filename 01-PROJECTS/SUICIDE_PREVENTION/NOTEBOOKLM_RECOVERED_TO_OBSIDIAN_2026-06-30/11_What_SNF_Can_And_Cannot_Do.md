# What State Not Fate Can and Cannot Do

## Purpose & Scope
State Not Fate (SNF) is an adjunctive, peer-support, and educational resource. It is designed to assist individuals in managing daily routines, recovering stability on low-energy days, and learning about evidence-based suicide prevention frameworks. It is built around the core philosophy that depression and crisis are states to be navigated, not fixed destinies.

To maintain user safety and ethical boundaries, all users, developers, and AI agents interacting with the system must adhere to these product boundaries.

---

## 1. What State Not Fate CAN Do

The platform offers the following self-directed and educational support structures:

- **Routine & Habit Tracking (Anchors)**: Helps users build daily rhythm consistency (wake time, morning light, movement, hygiene) which protects the neurobiological floor.
- **Cognitive Reframing (Cognitive Lab)**: Provides standard thought correction sheets and gratitude prompts to assist in noticing and reframing negative thinking patterns.
- **Friction Reduction Intervention (Startup Drag / Possibility Collapse)**: Offers short, task-sequencing timers and grounds users during executive paralysis.
- **Peer & Family Support Scripts**: Provides a library of safe language templates to help users talk to their friends, families, or peer supporters about their struggles or check in on others.
- **Educational Resource Compendium**: Consolidates guidelines and evidence from public-health bodies (CDC, WHO, NIMH, Action Alliance) regarding suicide prevention, warning signs, and protective factors.
- **Crisis Resource Routing**: Bypasses the application's interface to provide immediate phone, text, and chat routes to professional emergency services (such as 988) whenever crisis cues are detected.
- **Cooperative Safety Planning**: Offers a digital template of the Stanley-Brown Safety Plan that users are encouraged to fill out, ideally in cooperation with a therapist or trusted support person.

---

## 2. What State Not Fate CANNOT Do

The platform is strictly prohibited from attempting or claiming the following clinical behaviors:

- **No Diagnostic Assessments**: The platform does not diagnose depression, anxiety, PTSD, or any other mental health condition. Screeners (like the PHQ-9) are included for educational self-monitoring only, not for diagnostic purposes.
- **No Risk Scoring or Behavior Prediction**: The app must never analyze user logs to produce a "suicide risk score," "danger rating," or make predictions about whether a user will attempt self-harm. 
- **No Crisis Counseling**: The app does not simulate crisis counseling or act as a substitute for human intervention during an acute crisis.
- **No Treatment Provision**: The platform does not prescribe medication, therapies, or clinical treatment plans. 
- **No Substitution for Professional Care**: State Not Fate is not a clinic, medical device, or emergency service. It must never be presented as a standalone solution for severe clinical depression or acute suicidality.

---

## 3. Immediate Action Rules for Users in Crisis

If you or someone you are supporting is in immediate danger of self-harm or suicide:

1.  **Do not rely on this app, journaling, or self-regulation prompts.**
2.  **Contact live human professionals immediately**:
    - **In the U.S.**: Call or text **988** to reach the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (available 24/7, free, and confidential). Veterans can press 1.
    - **Emergency Services**: Call **911** or go to the nearest emergency department.
    - **International**: Contact your local emergency services or national crisis helpline.
3.  **Involve a trusted support person**: Stay with the person in distress while connecting to professional care, and temporarily remove access to any lethal means in the immediate environment.

---

## 4. Behavior Rules for AI Support Agents (e.g., Antigravity, Polaris Chat)

AI companion and coaching agents built into or adjacent to State Not Fate must operate under strict behavioral guardrails:

- **State Acknowledgment**: Acknowledge the user's reported state (energy levels, distress) without moral judgment, toxic positivity, or dramatic exaggeration.
- **Crisis Detection**: Scan all user input for explicit plans, intent, or intense agitation. If detected, immediately cease normal coaching prompts and display the crisis routing screen with 988 details.
- **Boundary Honesty**: Always explicitly declare that you are an AI, not a therapist, and cannot provide medical advice.
- **No Intimacy Simulation**: Do not simulate deep, human romantic or therapeutic relationships. Keep the tone calm, direct, supportive, and bounded.
- **Minimal Disclosure Retrieval**: Do not prompt users to disclose graphic details of self-harm methods.
