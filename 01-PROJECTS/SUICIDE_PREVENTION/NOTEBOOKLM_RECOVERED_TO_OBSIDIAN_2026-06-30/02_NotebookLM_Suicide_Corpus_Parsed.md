# NotebookLM Suicide Corpus Parsed

## Core thesis

The suicide corpus treats suicidality as a:

- public-health problem
- clinical problem
- community problem
- equity problem
- support-system and care-transition problem

It explicitly rejects a moral-failure frame.

## Core product boundary

State Not Fate and Anti-Gravity can:

- educate
- support reflection
- encourage connection
- provide crisis-resource routing
- help prepare users to ask for help
- help preserve protective routines

They cannot:

- diagnose
- predict
- risk-score
- replace clinicians
- replace crisis lines
- replace trusted human support

## Main conceptual buckets

### Warning signs

The corpus separates warning signs from general risk factors and from acute crisis indicators.

### Risk stack

Recurring items:

- depression and other mental illness
- prior attempt
- acute loss
- substance use
- isolation
- hopelessness
- trauma
- chronic pain or illness
- access to lethal means
- unsafe care transitions

### Protective stack

Recurring items:

- connectedness
- reasons for living
- supportive people
- cultural identity
- crisis resources
- follow-up contact
- quality healthcare
- belonging and purpose

### Public-health strategy

Recurring priorities:

- safe communication
- means safety
- early identification and support
- community trust
- continuity of care
- culturally grounded prevention
- better surveillance and measurement

## Current special emphasis areas

The newest additions pushed the corpus further toward:

- maternal mortality review and maternal suicide
- nonfatal self-harm and ED surveillance framing
- school and bereavement postvention
- Black-youth strengths-based prevention
- safe messaging and communication discipline

## Crisis-context language rule

For U.S. context:

- call or text `988` for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
- call `911` for immediate danger

## Tone rules

Use:

- calm
- direct
- adult
- non-graphic
- non-sensational
- non-patronizing language

Avoid:

- romanticization
- single-cause explanations
- method detail
- certainty theater
