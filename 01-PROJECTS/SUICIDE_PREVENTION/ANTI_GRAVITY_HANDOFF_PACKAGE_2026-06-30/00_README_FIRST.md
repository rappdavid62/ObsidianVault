Prepared: 2026-06-30
Audience: Anti-Gravity / AI execution systems working on State Not Fate, depression-recovery architecture, and the suicide-prevention compendium.

## What this package is

This package is a compact handoff built from:

- the local DOV handoff and State Not Fate files
- the current suicide-compendium carry-forward memory
- AI Wisebase retrieval results that reflect already-ingested source packets and project bundles
- the currently accessible Google Drive connector surface

It is not a clinical protocol, not crisis counseling, and not a license for an AI to diagnose, assess, predict, or claim to prevent suicide.

For U.S. crisis context: call or text `988` for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Call `911` for immediate danger.

## Bottom line

Anti-Gravity should treat the suicide corpus as a prevention-adjacent education and safety-design layer around State Not Fate, not as a clinical authority. The strongest durable frame is:

- depression as a state and systems failure, not a fate
- suicidality as a public-health, clinical, community, and equity problem
- Anti-Gravity as an execution operator and support scaffold
- proof, anchors, restartability, and connection as the central design language
- crisis routing and human support as non-negotiable boundaries
