# Box Breathing Circadian Reset

## Source Link
[https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5455070/](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5455070/)

## Curated Summary
Box breathing (or four-square breathing) is a somatic technique used to regulate the autonomic nervous system, lowering heart rate and reducing cortisol spikes.

## Suggested Exercises / Protocols
1. Inhale deeply through your nose for 4 seconds.
2. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
3. Exhale completely through your mouth for 4 seconds.
4. Hold your lungs empty for 4 seconds.
Repeat for 4 to 8 cycles to reset vagal tone.

## Anecdotes / Recovery Context
I use this breathing technique before making big decisions or when startup drag makes it impossible to work. It clears the physiological fog in 2 minutes.
