# Circadian Lock & Light Exposure Protocol

## Source Link
[https://statenotfate.netlify.app](https://statenotfate.netlify.app)

## Curated Summary
Getting direct outdoor sunlight into your eyes (without looking directly at the sun) within 1 hour of waking activates the master clock (SCN), locking in melatonin onset for night sleep.

## Suggested Exercises / Protocols
1. Go outside within 30 minutes of waking.
2. View direct sunlight (not through a window) for 10 minutes on sunny days, 20-30 minutes on cloudy days.
3. Keep your immediate environment bright during the day and dim after 8 PM.

## Anecdotes / Recovery Context
Fixing the morning light anchor was the single floor win that kept my biological engine running even when my mental state collapsed.
