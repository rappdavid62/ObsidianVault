# Crisis Safety Planning Template (Stanley-Brown)

## Source Link
[https://suicidepreventionlifeline.org](https://suicidepreventionlifeline.org)

## Curated Summary
A structured safety planning tool developed by Stanley and Brown to help individuals manage suicidal urges by identifying warning signs, coping strategies, and support systems.

## Extracted Contacts
- Crisis Text Line (Text HOME to 741741)
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (1-800-273-8255)

## Suggested Exercises / Protocols
1. Identify personal warning signs of a crisis.
2. List internal coping strategies (distractions, meditation).
3. List people and social settings that provide distraction.
4. List people I can ask for help.
5. List professionals and agencies to contact.
6. Make my environment safe (remove lethal means).

## Anecdotes / Recovery Context
Having a physical safety plan on the fridge takes away the decision-making load during acute distress when my brain ceases to function logically.
