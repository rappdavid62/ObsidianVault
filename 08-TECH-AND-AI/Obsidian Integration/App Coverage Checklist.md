## Definition Of Done

- Capture: a real item can be sent into the vault.
- Metadata: the note has `source_app`, `source_url` or `source_id`, and `captured_at`.
- Linkback: the note can reopen the source app item or location.
- Retrieval: the app workflow can use the note later.
